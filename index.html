What is iGEM?

iGEM Guelph is an internationally competitive synthetic biology team that competes in the annual Internationally Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) Grand Jamboree hosted in Paris, France. The competition challenges students around the world to solve real-world problems ranging from oncology to fashion using synthetic biology. The interdisciplinary nature of this competition requires teams to expand outside the lab to disciplines ranging from education, entrepreneurship, research stewardship, biosecurity, and social sciences. Requiring the recruitment of multi-faceted and dedicated team members.

More about iGEM Guelph

iGEM Guelph is one of Canada’s most well known iGEM teams, established in 2016. Our 9 years in the game have boasted us (x) silver and (x) gold medals, and with your support, we’ll be subject to many more.
