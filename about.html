Wet Lab

The Wet Lab team aims to complete a novel synthetic biology project, ensuring adherence to laboratory safety, the scientific method, and responsible research practices. We are committed to creating a space that allows individuals to build experience in microbiology and molecular biology techniques while having fun and connecting with others.

Dry Lab

The Dry Lab team puts the dream into the machine. The engineering working group builds the hardware that integrates our engineered organism into the solution. The modelling & software working group seek ways to optimize the operation to fruition.

Collaboration

Collaboration at iGEM is the department that is a jack-of-all-trades and yet master of all. Their responsibilities include connecting with other iGEM Teams and organizations to collaborate on projects, hosting events, executing scientific education initiatives, and operating the social media of our team. These creative individuals combine their love for science with the broader community.

Human Practices

Human Practices envisions the world we want to build through research and connecting with stakeholders, they are representatives from each department that make the dream come true. They thoughtfully review the impact the world has on our project and assess how the results will influence the wider world. This working group collaborates between departments to assemble academics, subject matter experts, and community members to gather diverse perspectives, ensuring a responsible project.
